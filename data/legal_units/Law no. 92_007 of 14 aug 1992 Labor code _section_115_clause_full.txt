Section 115 : Every employer, whether public or private and irrespective of the nature of this
activity, shall furnish the local labour inspectorate and the services in charge of employment with
detailed information concerning the situation of the manpower employed by him. Such
information shall be in the form of a declaration, the frequency and conditions of submission of
which shall be determined by order of the minister in charge of labour issued after consultation
with the National Labour Advisory Board.
