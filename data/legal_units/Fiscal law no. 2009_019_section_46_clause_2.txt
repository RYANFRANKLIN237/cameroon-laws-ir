(2) The rate of discharge tax shall be established by regional and
local authorities benefiting from the proceeds of this tax, within a bracket
determined by category of activities as follows:
Categories:
- A: from 0 to 20,000 francs
- B: from 21,001 to 40,000 francs
- C: from 41,001 to 50,000 francs
- D: from 51,001 to 100,000 francs
Councils may classify activities falling under each category and apply specific
rates thereto within the relevant bracket.
