(1) A National Commission for the Final Counting of Votes is
hereby set up. It shall be composed as follows:
Chairperson: a member of the Constitutional Council, appointed by the
President of the Constitutional Council;
Members:
- 2 (two) Judicial Officers appointed by the Chief Justice of the Supreme
Court;
- 5 (five) representatives of the Administration, appointed by the Minister
in charge of territorial administration;
- 5 (five) representatives of Elections Cameroon, appointed by the
Director General of Elections;
- 1 (one) representative of each candidate or political party taking part in
the election, appointed by the candidate or his political party.
