SECTION 281 : Slight Harm
Whoever by force or interference causes intentionally or unintentionally to another any .sickness or
inability to work lasting for more than 8 (eight) days and up to 30 (thirty) days shall be punished with
imprisonment for from 6 (six) days to 2 (two) years or with fine of from CFAF 5 000 (five thousand)
to CFAF 50 000 (fifty thousand}, or with both such imprisonment and fine.
