11
(a) At the opening of its first ordinary session, the National Assembly
shall elect its President and Bureau members.
(b) The National Assembly shall, during one of its sessions, adopt the State
budget. Where such budget is not adopted before the end of the current financial
year, the President of the Republic shall be empowered to extend the previous
budget by one-twelfth until a new one is passed.
