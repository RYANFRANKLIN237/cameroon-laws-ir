(4) Damages shall be assessed with due regard to all factors indicating that prejudice has been
caused and all factors determining the extent of such prejudice, and in particular, with due regard
:
(a) Where the worker is responsible, to his qualification and post;
(b) Where the employer is responsible, for whatsoever the type of employment, the
worker's seniority with the employer, his age and any vested rights.
However, the damages shall not be less than three months' salary or more than one month's
salary per year of service in the enterprise.
