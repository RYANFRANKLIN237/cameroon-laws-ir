SECTION 68: Upon its entry into force, this law shall repeal all previous
provisions, particularly those of Law No. 2004/16 of 22 July 2004 relating to
the establishment, organization and functioning of the National Commission
on Human Rights and Freedoms and those of Law No. 2010/4 of 13 April
2010 to amend and supplement some provisions of Law No. 2004/16 of 22
July 2004 relating to establishment, organization and functioning of the
National Commission on Human Rights and Freedoms.
