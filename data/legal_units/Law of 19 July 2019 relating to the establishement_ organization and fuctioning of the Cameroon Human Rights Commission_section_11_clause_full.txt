SECTION 11: The authorities in charge of places of detention may
oppose the Commission's visit only on serious and overriding grounds
relating to national defence, safety, security and public order and serious
disturbances in the places visited, on condition that they provide the
Commission with reasons for their opposition with a view to scheduling a
new date for the visit.
ln such cases, the officers concerned shall provide minimum
written information on the status of detainees in their institution.
