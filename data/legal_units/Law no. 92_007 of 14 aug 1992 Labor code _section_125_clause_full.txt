Section 125 : An order of the minister in charge of labour issued after consultation with the
National Advisory Board shall determine :
(a) the number of staff representatives to be elected and their division into electoral
colleges ;
(b) the procedure of election which shall be by secret ballot;
(c) the form of the report on the election which the employer shall be bound to submit to
the Labour Inspector of the area.
(d) the conditions under which the staff representatives shall be received by their employer
or his representative as well as the facilities to be made available to them;
(e) the conditions whereby a staff representative may be removed by the electoral college
that elected him.
