(3) Both Houses of Parliament shall meet on the same dates:
(a) in ordinary session during the months of March, June and November
each year, when convened by the Bureaux of the National Assembly and the
Senate, after consultation with the President of the Republic;
(b) in extraordinary session, at the request of the President of the Republic
or of one-third of the members of both Houses.
However, the two Houses shall be convened simultaneously only if the
business of the day concerns both of them.
