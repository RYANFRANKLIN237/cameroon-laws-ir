(1) The rates of impoundment fees shall be fixed within the
following maximum limits:
- heavy livestock: from 5,000 to 10,000 francs per head and per day;
- small livestock: from 2 000 to 5 000 francs per head and per day;
- pets : from 2 000 to 5 000 francs per head and per day;
- heavy vehicles and equipment : from 10,000 to 50,000 per vehicle and
per day;
- other vehicles : from 5,000 to 15,000 per vehicle and per day;
- motorcycles: from 1 000 to 5 000 francs per motorcycle and per day;
- other objects : from 1 000 to 3 000 francs per object and per day.
