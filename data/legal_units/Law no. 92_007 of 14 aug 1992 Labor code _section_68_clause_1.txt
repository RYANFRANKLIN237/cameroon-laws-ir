(1) Save in the case of trades and occupations where the established custom is to
provided for a different frequency of payment, which trades and occupations shall be specified
by an order of the minister in charge of labour issued after consultation with the National Labour
Advisory Board, wages shall be paid at regular intervals not exceeding one month.
However, workers may, at their request, receive at the end of fifteen days a payment on account
equal to half the monthly amount of their basic remuneration and in such case the balance due to
them shall be settled at the time of the following payment.
