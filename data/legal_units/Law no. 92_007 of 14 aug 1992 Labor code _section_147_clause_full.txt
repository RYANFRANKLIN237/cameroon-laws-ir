Section 147 : All orders and judgments and all official copies of contracts and any other
documents in respect of which execution may be ordered shall be marked with the executory
formula and healed as follows :
"Republic Cameroon" "In the name of the people of Cameroon" and shall close with the
following words :
"Therefore the president of the Republic of Cameroon commands and orders all bailiffs and
process-servers by these presents to give effect order (or judgment, etc.) and all procureurs
general, state counsels and all members of the judicial and Legal Service and civil servants
charged with taking action on behalf ; of the Republic to lend their aid when so required by law".
"In faith whereof the present order (or judgment, etc.) has been signed by the president and the
registrar".
