(1) Every owner of a firearm shall, on the declaration of such
owner no later than 15 March of each year, be required to pay an annual tax to
the council of the place where such firearm is located, in accordance with the
following rates:
- trade gun 2000 francs;
- smooth barrel sporting shot-gun 15,000 francs;
- gallery rifle 15,000 francs;
- rifled arm 2 000 francs;
- revolver and pistol 2000 francs.
