(1) The vote counting procedure shall be as follows:
a) The ballot box shall be opened and the number of envelopes contained
therein checked;
b) One of the scrutineers shall take the ballot paper out of each envelope,
unfold it and hand it to another scrutineer who shall read it aloud; the
name of the candidate or list of candidates, or the choice “YES” or “NO”
in case of referendum, appearing on the ballot paper shall be recorded
by two scrutineers on specially prepared counting sheets. Where an
envelope contains several ballot papers, such ballot papers shall be
invalid if they are different; they shall count as one vote if they are
identical.
