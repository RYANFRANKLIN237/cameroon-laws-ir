Section 150 : A third party claiming to be owner of all or part of the property distrained may,
before the sale, submit his claim to the president of the court either orally or in writing. Where
the evidence produced and the arguments put forward so warrant, the president shall suspend the
sale of the articles and effects claimed and shall then summon the parties within eight days. After
hearing the parties, the president shall make an order for against the appropriation of the property
distrained.
